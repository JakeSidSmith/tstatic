tstatic.

Usage:
  tstatic <dir> [options]
  tstatic -h | --help
  tstatic --version

Options:
  -h --help     Show this screen.
  --version     Show version.
  -b <auth> --basic-auth=<auth>   Enable basic-auth.
  -i <ips> --ips=<ips>  Allowed IP addresses.
  -l --list-dir  List Directory.
  -o --opbeat  Enable Opbeat.
